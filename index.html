Introduction

RécycleGO is developed by Timothy Wong Siew Ruey. It provides recyclable item identification, location-based recommendations, and AI assistance. We are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. How We Collect and Use Personal Data

We may collect:
- Account information (email for login)
- Device information (model, OS)
- Log data (IP, usage)
- Location data (for nearby recycling)
- Camera data (for item recognition)
- AI chat input

2. Device Permissions

- Camera: for recognition
- Location: for recommendations
Permissions can be disabled anytime.

3. Children Protection

This app is not intended for children under 13.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when required by law or service providers.

5. Third-Party SDKs

We may use cloud or analytics services which process data under their own policies.

6. Data Security

We use security measures such as HTTPS.

7. Managing Your Data

You may access, correct, or delete your data and withdraw consent.

8. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and retained only as necessary.
